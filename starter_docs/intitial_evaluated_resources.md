## Metadata Legend

**Status Values:**
- **Active** - Current, accurate, recommended
- **Flagged** - Needs attention; potential staleness concerns
- **Legacy** - Outdated in some aspects; use with caution
- **Archived** - No longer recommended; historical reference only

**Content Types:**
- **Tool-Specific** - Focused on specific tools/APIs (review every 3 months)
- **Model-Dependent** - Tied to model capabilities (review every 4 months)
- **Pricing** - Cost figures and economics (review every 6 months)
- **Career** - Job market conditions (review every 6 months)
- **Conceptual** - Strategic frameworks (review every 12 months)

**Confidence Scores (1-5):**
- **5** - Essential, highly authoritative
- **4** - Strong, reputable, directly applicable
- **3** - Solid, useful for most learners

**Access Types:**
- **Free** - Full content accessible without payment (in Active Resources section)
- **Paid** - Requires payment for full access (in Paid Resources section)
- **2** - Marginal, notable gaps or concerns
- **1** - At risk, may need replacement soon
